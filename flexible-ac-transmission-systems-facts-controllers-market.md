<p><p>Flexible AC Transmission Systems (FACTS) controllers are devices used in power systems to enhance the controllability and stability of the grid. There are four main types of FACTS controllers: Series Controllers, Shunt Controllers, Shunt-Series Controllers, and Series-Series Controllers. Series Controllers are placed in series with the transmission line to control the voltage and power flow. Shunt Controllers are connected in parallel to the transmission line to regulate voltage. Shunt-Series Controllers combine the functionalities of both series and shunt controllers. Series-Series Controllers are used to regulate voltage and power flow simultaneously.</p></p>
