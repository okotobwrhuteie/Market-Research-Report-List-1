<p><p>The Exhaust Gas Constant Volume Sampler (CVS) market includes two main types: Manual (Single-Venturi System) and Automatic (Multi-Venturi System). The Manual system requires manual monitoring and adjustment of the venturi system for sampling exhaust gas, while the Automatic system automates this process with multiple venturis for more precise measurements. The Manual system is cost-effective and suitable for smaller operations, while the Automatic system offers higher accuracy and efficiency for larger-scale applications. Both systems play a crucial role in monitoring emissions and ensuring regulatory compliance.</p></p>
