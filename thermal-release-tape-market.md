<p><p>Thermal release tape is available in two main types: single coated adhesive tape and dual coated adhesive tape. Single coated adhesive tape features a layer of adhesive on one side, making it suitable for applications where only one side needs to adhere. On the other hand, dual coated adhesive tape has adhesive on both sides, providing a strong bond on both surfaces. Both types are widely used in various industries for temporary bonding, reworkable applications, and clean removal without leaving residue.</p></p>
