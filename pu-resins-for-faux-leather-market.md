<p><p>PU resins are commonly used in two types of markets for faux leather production: dry-process synthetic leather and wet-process synthetic leather. In dry-process synthetic leather, the resin is applied directly to a fabric substrate and then dried and cured to form a durable and flexible material. In wet-process synthetic leather, the resin is mixed with a solvent and applied to a fabric substrate, then allowed to dry and cure to create a soft and pliable material. Both types of markets utilize PU resins to create high-quality faux leather products.</p></p>
