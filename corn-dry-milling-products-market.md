<p><p>Corn Dry Milling Products encompass various types such as Corn Grits, Cornmeal, Corn Flour, DDGS, and Ethanol. Corn Grits are coarsely ground corn kernels, while Cornmeal is finely ground corn. Corn Flour is a fine powder milled from the endosperm of corn kernels. DDGS (Dried Distillers Grains with Solubles) are a byproduct of ethanol production, used as animal feed. Ethanol is a biofuel produced from corn, used as a renewable energy source. These products play crucial roles in food, feed, and fuel industries.</p></p>
