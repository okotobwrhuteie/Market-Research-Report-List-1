<p><p>Scraper Heat Exchangers Market includes two main types: Reciprocating Type and Rotating Type. </p><p>Reciprocating Type heat exchangers have scraper blades that move back and forth in a reciprocating motion to scrape off fouling materials from the heat transfer surfaces. This type is commonly used in industries where frequent cleaning is required.</p><p>Rotating Type heat exchangers have rotating scraper blades that continuously clean the heat transfer surfaces as they rotate. This type is ideal for applications where continuous cleaning is necessary to maintain efficiency.</p></p>
