<p><strong>The Sewage Sludge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Raw primary sludge</li><li>Secondary sludge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Raw primary sludge, also known as primary sludge, is the solid waste material generated during the initial wastewater treatment process. It consists of organic and inorganic solids that settle at the bottom of sedimentation tanks. Raw primary sludge is often used for land application or incineration.</p><p>Secondary sludge is produced during the secondary treatment process, where biological processes are used to further treat the wastewater. This sludge contains higher concentrations of organic matter and nutrients compared to primary sludge. It is typically used for biogas production, composting, or land application.</p></p>
