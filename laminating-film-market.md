<p><p>Laminating film is available in various types, including BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) laminating film, BOPET (Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate) laminating film, and BOPA (Biaxially Oriented Polyamide) laminating film. BOPP laminating film is known for its high clarity and durability, making it suitable for a wide range of applications. BOPET laminating film offers excellent tensile strength and heat resistance, making it ideal for packaging and printing. BOPA laminating film provides high barrier properties against oxygen and moisture, making it ideal for food packaging.</p></p>
