<p><p>Non Fused Safety Switch Disconnectors are available in two main types - Rotary and Knife-blade. </p><p>Rotary switch disconnectors are operated by turning a knob or lever, allowing for quick and easy disconnection of electrical circuits. These switches are often used in industrial settings where frequent maintenance is required. </p><p>Knife-blade switch disconnectors utilize a blade mechanism to physically separate the circuit, ensuring a reliable disconnection. These switches are commonly found in residential and commercial applications where safety is a top priority.</p></p>
