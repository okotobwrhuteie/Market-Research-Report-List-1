<p><p>Monolithic Type Photonic Integrated Circuit market consists of two main types: Passive Photonic Integrated Circuit and Active Photonic Integrated Circuit. </p><p>Passive Photonic Integrated Circuits do not require external power sources and are used for functions like signal routing and splitting. They are more cost-effective and simpler in design compared to Active Photonic Integrated Circuits. </p><p>Active Photonic Integrated Circuits, on the other hand, require external power to amplify or modulate light signals. They are commonly used in applications that require signal amplification or modulation.</p></p>
