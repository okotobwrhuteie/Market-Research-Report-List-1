<p><strong>The Photolithography Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ArF Immersion</li><li>ArF Dry</li><li>KrF</li><li>I-Line</li><li>G-Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photolithography Agent Market is categorized into various types based on the wavelength of the light source used. ArF Immersion utilizes a wavelength of 193 nm, enabling high resolution and increased depth of focus. ArF Dry also uses a 193 nm wavelength but without the immersion process. KrF relies on a wavelength of 248 nm and is primarily used in smaller production lines. I-Line employs a wavelength of 365 nm and is suitable for mid-range production. G-Line, with a wavelength of 436 nm, is utilized in older production lines but offers lower resolution compared to the other types.</p></p>
