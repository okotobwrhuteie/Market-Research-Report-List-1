<p><p>Flexible Hybrid Electronics (FHE) is a technology that combines flexible substrates, such as plastics or thin films, with traditional rigid electronic components to create lightweight and stretchable electronics. FHE offers numerous advantages, including reduced weight, increased durability, and the ability to conform to irregular shapes, making it ideal for applications in industries such as healthcare, automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics.</p><p>The global Flexible Hybrid Electronics (FHE) market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.6% during the forecast period.
