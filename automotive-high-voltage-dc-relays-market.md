<p><strong>The Automotive High Voltage DC Relays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Relay</li><li>Gas-filled Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive High Voltage DC Relays are crucial components in electric vehicles, performing the function of switching high voltage DC power circuits on and off. Two common types of high voltage DC relays are Vacuum Relays and Gas-filled Relays. Vacuum Relays use a sealed chamber to prevent gas breakdown at high voltages, offering high reliability and insulation properties. Gas-filled relays, on the other hand, use a gas such as nitrogen or sulfur hexafluoride to prevent arcing and provide reliable switching in high voltage applications.</p></p>
