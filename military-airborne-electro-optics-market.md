<p><p>The Military Airborne Electro-Optics market includes two main types: Multispectral Electro-Optics and Hyperspectral Electro-Optics. Multispectral Electro-Optics utilize sensors that capture images in multiple wavelengths of light, allowing for enhanced target detection and identification. On the other hand, Hyperspectral Electro-Optics use sensors that capture images in hundreds of narrow, contiguous spectral bands, providing detailed information about the composition of objects or surfaces. Both types of electro-optics play a crucial role in military airborne surveillance and reconnaissance operations.</p></p>
