<p><p>Tankless electric water heaters are becoming increasingly popular due to their energy efficiency and space-saving design. They heat water on demand, eliminating the need for a large storage tank. This type of water heater is ideal for smaller households or homes with limited space. </p><p>On the other hand, tank electric water heaters are the traditional type with a storage tank that continuously heats and stores water until it is needed. While they may be less energy efficient than tankless models, they are more affordable and suitable for larger households with higher hot water demands.</p></p>
