<p><p>Drilling and completion fluids are specially formulated liquids or gases used in drilling operations to lubricate the drill bit, carry the cuttings to the surface, and maintain the stability of the wellbore. The drilling and completion fluids market find applications in various sectors such as oil and gas, mining, and water well drilling.</p><p>The drilling and completion fluids market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing exploration and production activities in the oil and gas industry. Oil-based systems use oil as the base fluid, providing excellent lubrication and temperature stability.
