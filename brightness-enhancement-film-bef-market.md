<p><p>Brightness Enhancement Film (BEF) is a key technology in displays to improve screen brightness and energy efficiency. It comes in various types such as Normal Prism, Multi-function Prism, Micro Lens Film, and Dual Brightness Enhancement Film (DBEF). Normal Prism BEF is designed for basic brightness enhancement, while Multi-function Prism BEF offers additional functionalities. Micro Lens Film aims to enhance brightness and reduce power consumption. DBEF combines two layers of prisms for optimal performance. Each type caters to different display requirements and market demands.</p></p>
