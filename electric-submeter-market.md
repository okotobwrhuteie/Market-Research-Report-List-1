<p><ul><li>Current Transformer</li><li>Rogowski Coil</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric submeter market consists of three main types of submeters: current transformer (CT), Rogowski coil, and other submeters. Current transformer submeters work by using a transformer to measure the electrical current flowing through a conductor. Rogowski coil submeters use a flexible coil to measure electrical current, offering advantages such as ease of installation and accurate readings. Lastly, the "other" category includes various types of submeters, such as solid-core current transformers and split-core current transformers, which have their own unique characteristics and applications. These submeters are widely used in the energy industry for monitoring and managing electricity usage in different environments.</p></p>
