<p><ul><li>Single Layer Geomembranes</li><li>Double Layer Geomembranes</li><li>Three Layer Geomembranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single Layer Geomembranes are made of one layer of material, providing basic containment for various applications. Double Layer Geomembranes consist of two layers, offering enhanced strength and protection against leaks. Three Layer Geomembranes are the most advanced option, featuring an additional layer for increased durability and resistance to punctures. Each type caters to different project requirements, with single layer geomembranes being the most basic and three layer geomembranes offering the highest level of protection and longevity.</p></p>
