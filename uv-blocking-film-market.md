<p><p>UV Blocking Film is available in two main types: Clear UV Blocking Film and Dyed UV Blocking Film. Clear UV Blocking Film is a transparent film that provides protection from harmful UV rays while allowing visible light to pass through. On the other hand, Dyed UV Blocking Film is a tinted film that not only blocks UV rays but also reduces glare and provides some level of privacy. Both types of UV Blocking Film offer protection for various applications such as automotive, residential, and commercial use.</p></p>
