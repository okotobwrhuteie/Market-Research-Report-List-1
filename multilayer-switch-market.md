<p><strong>The Multilayer Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Layer 2 Switch</li><li>Layer 3 Switch</li><li>Layer 4 Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multilayer switches are categorized into three main types based on the layers of the OSI model that they operate on. Layer 2 switches operate at the data link layer and are primarily concerned with MAC addresses and Ethernet frames. Layer 3 switches function at the network layer, utilizing IP addresses for routing decisions. Layer 4 switches work at the transport layer and can make routing decisions based on TCP or UDP port numbers. Each type of switch offers specific functionalities and capabilities tailored to different network requirements.</p></p>
