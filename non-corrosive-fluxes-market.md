<p><p>Non-corrosive fluxes are used in soldering applications to remove oxides and improve the wetting of metals without causing corrosion. Resin flux is a type of non-corrosive flux that is composed of organic resins and activators which are non-conductive and do not leave a residue when heated. Organic fluxes are also non-corrosive and non-conductive, containing organic acid activators that promote solderability without damaging the metal surfaces. Both resin and organic fluxes are safe to use on sensitive electronic components and are widely used in the electronics industry.</p></p>
