<p><p>Plant based milk is a dairy-free alternative to traditional cow's milk that is made from various plant sources such as almonds, soy, oats, rice, and coconut. It caters to consumers who are lactose intolerant, have dairy allergies, follow a vegan diet, or simply prefer a plant-based diet. Plant based milk is known for being rich in vitamins and minerals, low in saturated fats, and cholesterol-free, making it a popular choice among health-conscious consumers.</p><p>The Plant Based Milk Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.00% during the forecast period. The market is being driven by the increasing awareness about the health benefits of plant based milk compared to traditional dairy milk. Moreover, the rising number of individuals adopting vegan or vegetarian diets is also contributing to the growth of the market. Additionally, the availability of various flavors and formulations of plant based milk products is attracting a wider consumer base.</p><p>The latest trends in the Plant Based Milk Market include the introduction of new plant sources such as hemp and quinoa, innovative packaging solutions, and the launch of fortified plant based milk products with added vitamins and minerals. The market is also witnessing a surge in demand for organic and non-GMO plant based milk products. As consumers become more health-conscious and environmentally aware, the plant based milk market is expected to continue its growth trajectory in the coming years.</p></p>
