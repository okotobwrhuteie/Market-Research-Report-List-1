<p><p>Eddy Current Proximity Sensors are available in two main types: split type and integrated type. Split type sensors are made up of separate transmitter and receiver coils, allowing for greater flexibility in installation and positioning. Integrated type sensors, on the other hand, combine both the transmitter and receiver coils into a single unit, providing a more compact and simplified design. Both types of sensors have their unique advantages and are used in various industrial applications for proximity sensing and measurement.</p></p>
