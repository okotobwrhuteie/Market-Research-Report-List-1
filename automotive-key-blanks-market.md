<p><p>Automotive Key Blanks can be categorized into different types based on their functionality and technology. Metal Head Automotive Key Blanks are traditional keys with a metal head that need to be physically inserted into the vehicle's ignition to start the engine. On the other hand, Transponder Technology Automotive Key Blanks use electronic transponder chips to communicate with the vehicle's immobilizer system, providing an extra layer of security against theft. Both types of key blanks play a crucial role in providing access to and starting modern vehicles.</p></p>
