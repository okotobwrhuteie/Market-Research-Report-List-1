<p><p>Vehicle Grade 3D LiDAR Sensor Market consists of two main types: Mechanical LiDAR and Solid State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR systems use moving parts to steer the laser beam and gather data, making them durable but potentially slower and more expensive. On the other hand, Solid State LiDAR systems eliminate the need for moving parts, resulting in faster, smaller, and cheaper sensors. Both types offer unique advantages and are being used in various applications within the automotive industry.</p></p>
