<p><p>Sintered Nd-Fe-B Magnets can be categorized into two main types based on their magnetization direction - Axially Magnetized and Diametrically Magnetized. Axially Magnetized magnets are oriented along the axis of the magnet, producing a strong magnetic field from north to south poles. Diametrically Magnetized magnets are oriented across the diameter of the magnet, creating a magnetic field from one side to the other. Each type has specific applications and advantages depending on the desired functionality and requirements of the magnet system.</p></p>
