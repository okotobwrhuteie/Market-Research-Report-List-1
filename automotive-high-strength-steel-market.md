<p><strong>The Automotive High Strength Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional HSS</li><li>AHSS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive high strength steel can be classified into two main types: Conventional HSS and Advanced High Strength Steel (AHSS). Conventional HSS refers to steel with a strength level of around 300-400 MPa, suitable for basic structural components in vehicles. On the other hand, AHSS includes steel grades with high strength levels ranging from 500-1000 MPa, offering improved crash performance, weight reduction, and fuel efficiency. AHSS is used in more advanced applications like bumpers, door reinforcements, and side impact beams in modern vehicles.</p></p>
