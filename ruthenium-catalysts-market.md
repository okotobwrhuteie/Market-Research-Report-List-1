<p><strong>The Ruthenium Catalysts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder</li><li>Particle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ruthenium catalysts are available in two main market types: powder and particle. Powder catalysts are finely divided ruthenium metal particles, usually used in homogeneous catalysis. They have high reactivity due to their large surface area. On the other hand, particle catalysts consist of ruthenium nanoparticles embedded in a supporting material, commonly used in heterogeneous catalysis. They offer good stability and can be easily separated from the reaction mixture. Both types play crucial roles in various industrial processes requiring catalytic reactions.</p></p>
