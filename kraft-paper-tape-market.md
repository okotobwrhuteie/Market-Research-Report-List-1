<p><p>Kraft paper tape is a type of packaging tape used to seal boxes and cartons. There are two main types in the market: Water-free Kraft Paper Tape and Wet Kraft Paper Tape. Water-free tape is activated by pressure alone, making it a more environmentally friendly option. It is ideal for sealing lightweight packages. Wet Kraft Paper Tape, on the other hand, requires water for activation and offers a stronger hold, making it suitable for sealing heavy-duty packages. Both types are commonly used in packaging and shipping industries.</p></p>
