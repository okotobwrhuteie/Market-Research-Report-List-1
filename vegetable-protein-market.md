<p><p>Vegetable protein market includes various types such as Wheat Protein, Soy Protein Concentrate, Soy Protein Isolate, Textured Soy Protein, and Pea Protein. Wheat protein is derived from wheat and is a good source of essential amino acids. Soy protein concentrate and isolate are extracted from soybeans and are high-quality proteins with a complete amino acid profile. Textured soy protein is a meat analogue made from defatted soy flour, providing a meat-like texture. Pea protein is derived from yellow peas and is a hypoallergenic source of protein.</p></p>
