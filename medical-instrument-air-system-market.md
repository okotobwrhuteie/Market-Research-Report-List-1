<p><strong>The Medical Instrument Air System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Duplex Systems</li><li>Triplex Systems</li><li>Quadplex Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Instrument Air System market offers different types of systems based on the number of air compressors used. Duplex systems utilize two compressors to maintain system functionality in case of maintenance or breakdown. Triplex systems use three compressors for increased reliability and efficiency. Quadplex systems, on the other hand, incorporate four compressors for even greater redundancy and capability. Each system type caters to different needs and preferences of healthcare facilities in ensuring continuous and high-quality supply of medical-grade compressed air.</p></p>
