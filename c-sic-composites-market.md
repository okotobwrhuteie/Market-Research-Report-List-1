<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The C-SiC Composites Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carbon Particles and Silicon Carbide (Cp/SiC) Composites</li><li>Carbon Fiber and Silicon Carbide (Cf/SiC) Composites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>C-SiC Composites are advanced materials that combine carbon particles or carbon fiber with silicon carbide to create high-performance materials with excellent mechanical and thermal properties. Carbon Particles and Silicon Carbide (Cp/SiC) Composites utilize carbon particles mixed with silicon carbide to enhance toughness and strength. On the other hand, Carbon Fiber and Silicon Carbide (Cf/SiC) Composites incorporate carbon fibers embedded in a silicon carbide matrix to provide high stiffness and lightweight characteristics. Both types of composites are widely used in aerospace, defense, and automotive industries.</p></p>
