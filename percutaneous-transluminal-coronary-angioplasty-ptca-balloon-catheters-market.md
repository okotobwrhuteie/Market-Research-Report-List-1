Normal Balloon Catheters inflate to open the artery, Drug Eluting Balloon Catheters release medication to prevent re-blockage, Cutting Balloon Catheters have blades to cut through hard plaques, and Scoring Balloon Catheters create small incisions in the plaque for better dilation.</p></p>
