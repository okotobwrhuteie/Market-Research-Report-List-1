<p><ul><li>Radiation Hardening by Design (RHBD)</li><li>Radiation Hardening by Process (RHBP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiation-hardened electronics market includes two main types: Radiation Hardening by Design (RHBD) and Radiation Hardening by Process (RHBP). RHBD involves designing electronic components with radiation-resistant materials and structures to withstand harsh environments. On the other hand, RHBP focuses on modifying the manufacturing process itself to make electronic components less susceptible to radiation-induced failures. Both types aim to improve the reliability and longevity of electronic devices used in industries such as aerospace, defense, and nuclear power.</p></p>
