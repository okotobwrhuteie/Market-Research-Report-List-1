<p><ul><li>DLP</li><li>LCD</li><li>LCoS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Interactive projectors come in three main types on the market: DLP, LCD, and LCoS. DLP projectors use a digital micromirror device to create images, providing high contrast and deep blacks. LCD projectors use liquid crystal displays to produce bright and vibrant colors. LCoS projectors utilize liquid crystal on silicon technology to deliver sharp images with high resolution. Each type has its own strengths and weaknesses, catering to different needs and preferences in the interactive projection market.</p></p>
