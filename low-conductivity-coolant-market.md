<p><p>Low conductivity coolant market includes two main types: single phase coolant and dual phase coolant. Single phase coolant is a type of coolant that consists of a single liquid phase, typically water mixed with additives to enhance its cooling properties. Dual phase coolant, on the other hand, is a coolant system that includes both liquid and gas phases, allowing for more efficient heat transfer. Both types of coolant are used in various industries to maintain optimal operating temperatures and prevent equipment damage due to overheating.</p></p>
