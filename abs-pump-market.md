<p><p>The ABS Pump market consists of two main types: Electronic ABS Pump and Mechanical ABS Pump. Electronic ABS Pump is a more advanced and modern type that utilizes electronic sensors to detect wheel speed and control braking pressure accordingly. Mechanical ABS Pump, on the other hand, relies on mechanical components such as valves and pumps to manage braking pressure. Both types serve the same purpose of preventing wheel lock-up during braking, but they differ in the technology they use to achieve this goal.</p></p>
