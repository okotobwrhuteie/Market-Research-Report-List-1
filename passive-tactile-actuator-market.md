<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Passive Tactile Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) Actuators</li><li>Linear Resonant Actuators (LRA)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive tactile actuators can be categorized into different types, including Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) Actuators, Linear Resonant Actuators (LRA), and Others. ERM actuators use a rotating mass off-center to generate vibrations, while LRA actuators rely on a linear mass-spring system for vibrating feedback. The "Others" category includes various additional types of passive tactile actuators that may use different mechanisms to provide haptic feedback. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in industries such as consumer electronics, gaming, automotive, and medical devices.</p></p>
