<p><p>Resuscitators and transport ventilators market types include continuous positive airway pressure (CPAP), noninvasive positive pressure ventilation (NIPPV), and others. CPAP is a type of therapy that uses mild air pressure to keep the airways open, commonly used for sleep apnea. NIPPV delivers air pressure through a mask, helping patients breathe without the need for intubation. Other types of ventilators may include high-frequency oscillatory ventilation or pressure support ventilation. Each type serves a specific purpose in assisting patients with breathing difficulties.</p></p>
