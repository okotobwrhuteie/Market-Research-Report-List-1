<p><p>Stained concrete is a popular choice for flooring due to its durability and aesthetic appeal. There are two main types of stained concrete markets: acid-based staining and water-based staining. Acid-based staining involves the use of acid to create a chemical reaction with the concrete, resulting in unique and variegated colors. Water-based staining, on the other hand, uses water-based dyes or pigments to penetrate the concrete and provide a more uniform color. Both types of staining offer a wide range of colors and design possibilities for various applications.</p></p>
