<p><p>The Petroleum Cracking Catalyst market consists of various types which include FCC Catalyst, Hydrogenation Catalyst, and Reforming Catalyst. FCC Catalyst is primarily used in fluid catalytic cracking processes to convert high-boiling hydrocarbons into lighter products. Hydrogenation Catalyst is employed in removing impurities from crude oil or improving the quality of various petroleum products. Reforming Catalyst is utilized in reforming processes for converting low-octane naphthas into high-octane fuels. Each type of catalyst plays a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and performance of petroleum refining processes.</p></p>
