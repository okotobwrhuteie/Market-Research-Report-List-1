<p><p>Electronically Commutated EC Motors can be categorized into two main types based on the position of the rotor within the motor: Inner Rotor Motor and Outer Rotor Motor. Inner Rotor Motors have the rotor positioned inside the stator, which allows for a more compact design suitable for applications with limited space. On the other hand, Outer Rotor Motors have the rotor positioned outside the stator, providing higher torque at lower speeds and making them ideal for high-performance applications.</p></p>
