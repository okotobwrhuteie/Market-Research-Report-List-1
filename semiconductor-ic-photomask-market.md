<p><p>Semiconductor IC photomask market is categorized into two main types - Quartz Mask and Soda Mask. Quartz Mask is made from high-quality quartz material, ensuring precision in semiconductor manufacturing processes. On the other hand, Soda Mask is made from soda lime glass, offering a cost-effective alternative for semiconductor production. Both types of photomasks play a crucial role in lithography processes by transferring circuit patterns onto semiconductor substrates accurately and efficiently. The choice between Quartz and Soda masks depends on factors like budget constraints and required precision in manufacturing.</p></p>
