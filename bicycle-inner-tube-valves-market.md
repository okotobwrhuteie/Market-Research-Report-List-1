<p><p>There are two main types of valves commonly used in bicycle inner tubes: rubber tire valves and metal tire valves. Rubber tire valves are made of flexible rubber and are often found on cheaper or standard bicycles. Metal tire valves, on the other hand, are made of durable materials like brass or aluminum and are typically seen on higher-end bikes or racing bikes. Both types of valves serve the same purpose of inflating and deflating the tire, but metal tire valves are considered more durable and long-lasting compared to rubber tire valves.</p></p>
