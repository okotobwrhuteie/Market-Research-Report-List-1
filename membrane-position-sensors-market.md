<p><p>Membrane position sensors are classified into two main types: linear and rotary. Linear membrane position sensors measure the linear movement of an object along a straight path, providing accurate position feedback. On the other hand, rotary membrane position sensors are designed to measure the rotary movement of an object around a central axis. Both types of sensors are extensively used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing to ensure precise positioning and control of mechanical systems.</p></p>
