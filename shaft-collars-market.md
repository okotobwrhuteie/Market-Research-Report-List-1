<p><p>Shaft collars are used to secure components in place on a shaft. There are two main types of shaft collars on the market: clamping shaft collars and set screw shaft collars. Clamping shaft collars utilize a screw mechanism to securely clamp onto the shaft, providing a strong and reliable hold. On the other hand, set screw shaft collars feature a small screw that is tightened against the shaft to hold it in place. Both types of shaft collars are widely used in various industries for different applications.</p></p>
