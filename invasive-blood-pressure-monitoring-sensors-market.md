<p><p>Invasive blood pressure monitoring sensors come in three main types: single channel sensor, dual channel sensor, and triple channel sensor. Single channel sensors measure only one parameter, typically arterial blood pressure. Dual channel sensors monitor two parameters, such as arterial and venous blood pressure simultaneously. Triple channel sensors are capable of measuring three parameters, such as arterial blood pressure, venous blood pressure, and cardiac output. Each type offers different levels of monitoring capability and may be used in various medical settings based on the specific needs of the patient.</p></p>
