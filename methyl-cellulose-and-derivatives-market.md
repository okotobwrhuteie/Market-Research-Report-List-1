<p><p>Methyl cellulose and derivatives are cellulose ethers that are widely used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, construction, personal care, and cosmetics. These properties make methyl cellulose and derivatives ideal for applications such as tablet coatings in the pharmaceutical industry, as well as in food products like ice creams and sauces.</p><p>The global market for methyl cellulose and derivatives is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.2% during the forecast period. Additionally, the growing construction industry is also a key factor contributing to the market growth, as methyl cellulose derivatives are utilized in dry-mix mortars for better workability and adhesion.</p><p>Overall, the rising demand for methyl cellulose and derivatives across various industries, coupled with technological advancements and product innovations, is expected to drive the market growth in the coming years.</p></p>
