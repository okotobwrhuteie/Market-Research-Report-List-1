<p><p>Indoor disinfectant market is categorized into two main types: non-oxidizing disinfectants and oxidizing disinfectants. Non-oxidizing disinfectants work by disrupting the cell membrane of microorganisms, effectively killing them. Examples include quaternary ammonium compounds and phenolics. On the other hand, oxidizing disinfectants work by releasing oxygen radicals that destroy the cell walls of microorganisms. Common examples include chlorine-based disinfectants like bleach. Both types are essential for maintaining a clean and sanitized indoor environment, with each having its own unique properties and applications.</p></p>
