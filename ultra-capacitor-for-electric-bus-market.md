<p><p>Ultra-capacitors for electric buses are categorized into three main types - Electrochemical Double Layer Capacitors, Hybrid Capacitors, and Pseudo Capacitors. Electrochemical Double Layer Capacitors store energy through an electrostatic double layer at the electrode-electrolyte interface. Hybrid Capacitors combine features of both ultra-capacitors and batteries, providing high energy density and power density. Pseudo Capacitors utilize Faradaic reactions to store energy, offering high energy density. Each type has its own unique characteristics and advantages, catering to different needs in the electric bus market.</p></p>
