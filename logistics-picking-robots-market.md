<p><p>The logistics picking robots market can be divided into two main types: full-automatic and semi-automatic. Full-automatic robots are capable of completing the entire picking process without human intervention, from identifying items to picking and packing them. On the other hand, semi-automatic robots require some human input and supervision throughout the picking process. Full-automatic robots are best suited for high-volume operations with minimal human involvement, while semi-automatic robots are ideal for tasks that require more flexibility and adaptability.</p></p>
