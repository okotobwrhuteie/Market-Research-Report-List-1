<p><p>Soft Piezoelectric Ceramics Market Types - PZT5A and PZT5H are two common types of piezoelectric materials used in various applications. PZT5A is known for its high strain levels and good temperature stability, making it suitable for sensors and transducers. On the other hand, PZT5H offers high dielectric constant and high electromechanical coupling coefficient, making it ideal for actuators and ultrasound transducers. Both materials have their own unique properties and are chosen based on specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
