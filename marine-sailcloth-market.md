Woven sailcloth is made from interwoven fibers, providing strength and stability. Taffeta sailcloth has a smooth, shiny finish and is often used for racing sails. Polyester sailcloth is cost-effective and easy to maintain, making it a popular choice for recreational sailors. Each type offers unique characteristics to suit different sailing needs.</p></p>
