<p><p>Commercial pool cleaning robots come in two main market types: Crawler Drive and Wheel Drive. Crawler Drive robots operate on tracks, allowing them to easily maneuver over obstacles and provide better traction for climbing walls and steps. They are ideal for larger pools with complex shapes. Wheel Drive robots, on the other hand, use wheels to move around the pool. They are better suited for smaller, simpler pools and are known for their agility and ease of operation. Both types offer efficient cleaning and maintenance solutions for commercial pool owners.</p></p>
