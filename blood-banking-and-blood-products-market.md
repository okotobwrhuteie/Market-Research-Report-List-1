RBCs are used to restore oxygen-carrying capacity in patients with anemia. Platelets are essential for clotting and are often transfused to patients with bleeding disorders. Plasma is rich in clotting factors and is used to treat clotting disorders. Cryoprecipitate contains specific clotting proteins and is used for patients with deficiencies. Whole blood is sometimes transfused in emergency situations for massive blood loss.</p></p>
