<p><p>Boom mounted mulchers are available in two main types: Cupped Cutter and Square Cutter. Cupped Cutter mulchers feature curved blades that are angled to provide a slicing action, ideal for cutting through dense vegetation and providing a finer mulch. On the other hand, Square Cutter mulchers have straight blades that are more suitable for chopping through thicker brush and tree limbs. Both types offer different cutting styles and are designed to provide efficient mulching solutions for various landscaping and land clearing applications.</p></p>
