<p><p>SUS-Al-alloy Clad Metals are classified into two market types based on their structure: Bilayer Structure and Three-layer Structure. In Bilayer Structure, the metal consists of two layers, with one being SUS (Stainless-Steel) and the other being Al-alloy. This structure provides good corrosion resistance and mechanical stability. In Three-layer Structure, an additional layer of material is added between the SUS and Al-alloy layers for improved bonding and strength. Both market types offer unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
