<p><p>PGA resin is available in two main market types: industrial grade and medical grade. Industrial grade PGA resin is used in various industries for applications such as packaging, adhesives, coatings, and textiles. It is valued for its high strength, biodegradability, and versatility in manufacturing processes. On the other hand, medical grade PGA resin is specifically designed for use in medical devices and implants due to its biocompatibility, resorbability, and sterilizability properties. Both industrial and medical grade PGA resin offer unique benefits and serve different market demands.</p></p>
