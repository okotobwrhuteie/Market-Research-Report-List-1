<p><ul><li>Side-by-side Type</li><li>Stacked-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluid Catalytic Cracking (FCC) units come in two main types: side-by-side type and stacked-type. Side-by-side type units are characterized by their compact design, where the regenerator and reactor are placed next to each other horizontally. On the other hand, stacked-type units have a vertical configuration, with the regenerator situated on top of the reactor. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages in terms of efficiency, space utilization, and ease of maintenance, catering to different market preferences.</p></p>
