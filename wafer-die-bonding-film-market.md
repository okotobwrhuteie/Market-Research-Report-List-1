<p><p>Wafer die bonding film comes in two main types: non-conductive and conductive. Non-conductive types are used when electrical isolation between the die and the substrate is required, preventing any electrical connection between them. On the other hand, conductive types are used when electrical conductivity is necessary, allowing for electrical connection between the die and the substrate. Each type serves different purposes based on the specific requirements of the application in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
