<p><p>Grain and Seed Cleaning Machines come in two main types: Pre-Cleaning Type and Fine Cleaning Type. Pre-Cleaning Type machines are designed to remove large impurities like stones, sticks, and bulky seeds from grains and seeds. On the other hand, Fine Cleaning Type machines are used for a more detailed cleaning process, removing smaller impurities like dust, chaff, and broken seeds. Both types of machines are essential in ensuring the quality and purity of grains and seeds before further processing.</p></p>
