<p><ul><li>Hydrocolloid</li><li>Gums</li><li>Stabilizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food texturants market consists of different types such as hydrocolloids, gums, and stabilizers which are used to modify and improve the texture of food products. Hydrocolloids like pectin and carrageenan are water-soluble polymers that provide thickening and gelling properties. Gums like guar and xanthan gum are used as stabilizers and emulsifiers. Stabilizers are substances like starches and proteins that improve the texture and shelf-life of food products. These texturants play a crucial role in enhancing the mouthfeel and overall quality of food products.</p></p>
