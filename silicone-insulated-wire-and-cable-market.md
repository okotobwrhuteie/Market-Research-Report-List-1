<p><ul><li>Single Core</li><li>Multi-core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone insulated wire and cable market types include single core and multi-core. Single core wires consist of a single conductor surrounded by silicone insulation, suitable for applications requiring simple connections. Multi-core wires have multiple conductors within the same insulation, ideal for more complex circuits. Both types offer flexibility and high temperature resistance, making them popular choices in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics. The decision between single core and multi-core depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
