<p><p>5G Modules for IoT market types include LGA Package, Mini PCIe Package, M.2 Package, and Others. LGA Package modules are surface-mounted and soldered directly onto a circuit board. Mini PCIe Package modules plug into the Mini PCIe slot on a motherboard. M.2 Package modules are inserted into the M.2 slot on a motherboard. Other types of 5G modules may include solderable modules, industrial-grade modules, or automotive-grade modules, each offering their own unique features and applications in the IoT industry.</p></p>
