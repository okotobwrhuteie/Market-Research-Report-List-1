<p><p>Sealing gaskets are used to prevent leaks and ensure a tight seal between two surfaces. The market for sealing gaskets can be classified into two main types: metallic and non-metallic. Metallic gaskets are made of materials like stainless steel and are known for their high durability and resistance to extreme temperatures and pressures. Non-metallic gaskets, on the other hand, are made of materials such as rubber, graphite, and PTFE and are preferred for their flexibility, ability to conform to irregular surfaces, and resistance to chemicals.</p></p>
