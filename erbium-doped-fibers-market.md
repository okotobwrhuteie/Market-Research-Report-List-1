<p><p>Erbium-Doped Fibers market is segmented into SM Fiber and PM Fiber types. SM Fiber (Single-Mode Fiber) is designed for transmitting a single mode of light, offering a higher bandwidth and longer transmission distances. PM Fiber (Polarization-Maintaining Fiber) maintains the polarization of light signal, crucial for applications requiring precise control over the light's polarization. Both types are utilized in various industries including telecommunications, data communication, and fiber lasers, catering to specific needs and requirements of different applications.</p></p>
