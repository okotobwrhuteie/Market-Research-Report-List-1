<p><p>The High Performance Insulated Substrates for Power Modules market includes two main types: AlN DBC Substrate and Al2O3 DBC Substrate. AlN DBC Substrates are made of aluminum nitride and offer high thermal conductivity, excellent dielectric properties, and low thermal expansion. On the other hand, Al2O3 DBC Substrates are composed of aluminum oxide and provide good thermal performance and electrical insulation. Both types of substrates are commonly used in power modules for their superior thermal management capabilities.</p></p>
