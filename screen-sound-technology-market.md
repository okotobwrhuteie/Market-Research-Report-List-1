<p><p>Screen Sound Technology refers to the use of various methods to produce sound directly from a screen or display device. Cantilever Piezoelectric Ceramic Sound technology utilizes piezoelectric ceramic elements attached to the screen to create sound waves. On the other hand, Screen Exciter Sound technology involves the use of exciters or transducers mounted on the screen to generate sound vibrations. Both market types offer innovative solutions for integrating sound into displays, providing immersive audio experiences for users.</p></p>
