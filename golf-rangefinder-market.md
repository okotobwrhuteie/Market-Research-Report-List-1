<p><ul><li>Laser Golf Rangefinders</li><li>GPS Golf Rangefinders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Golf rangefinders are available in two main types: Laser golf rangefinders and GPS golf rangefinders. Laser rangefinders use laser technology to measure the distance between the device and a target on the golf course, providing precise yardage readings. GPS rangefinders, on the other hand, use satellite technology to map out the course and provide distances to various points on the course. Both types of rangefinders are popular among golfers for improving accuracy and performance on the course.</p></p>
