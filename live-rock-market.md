Aquacultured live rock is considered more sustainable and environmentally friendly as it is produced in controlled environments without disturbing natural marine ecosystems. Moreover, aquacultured live rock is free from pests and diseases commonly found in wild-collected live rock, making it a preferred choice among aquarium hobbyists.
