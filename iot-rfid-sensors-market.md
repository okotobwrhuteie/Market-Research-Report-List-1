<p><strong>The IoT RFID Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency Sensors</li><li>High Frequency Sensors</li><li>UHF Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IoT RFID sensors market consists of three main types of sensors based on their frequency range: Low Frequency Sensors, High Frequency Sensors, and UHF Sensors. Low frequency sensors operate at frequencies below 135 kHz, suitable for short-range communication. High frequency sensors operate at frequencies between 3 MHz and 30 MHz, ideal for medium-range communication. UHF sensors operate at frequencies between 300 MHz and 3 GHz, providing long-range communication capabilities. Each type of sensor caters to specific use cases and environments within the IoT RFID market.</p></p>
