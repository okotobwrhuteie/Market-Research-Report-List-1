<p><p>Medium Density Composite Panels are classified into three main types based on their specific properties and applications. Moisture resistant MDF is designed to withstand exposure to moisture without deteriorating, making it ideal for use in humid environments such as bathrooms and kitchens. Flame retardant MDF is treated with chemicals to reduce its flammability, making it suitable for applications where fire safety is a concern. General MDF is the standard type used for a wide range of interior applications due to its versatility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
