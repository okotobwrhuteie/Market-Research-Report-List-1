<p><p>Military surveillance drones are classified into three main types based on their design and functionality. Fixed-wing drones are typically larger and have longer flight times, making them ideal for long-range reconnaissance missions. Rotary blade drones are smaller and more agile, making them suitable for close-range surveillance and tactical operations. Hybrid drones combine features of both fixed-wing and rotary blade drones, offering a balance between endurance and maneuverability. Each type of drone caters to different military surveillance needs and requirements.</p></p>
