<p><p>Stereo headphones are available in two main types - In-Ear Headphones and On-Ear Headphones. In-Ear headphones are small and lightweight, fitting snugly inside the ear canal, providing a more immersive audio experience and great portability. On the other hand, On-Ear headphones have larger ear cups that sit on the ears, providing more comfort during longer listening sessions and better sound isolation. Both types cater to different preferences and needs of consumers, offering a wide range of options in the stereo headphones market.</p></p>
