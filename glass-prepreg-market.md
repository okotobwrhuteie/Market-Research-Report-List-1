<p><ul><li>Thermosetting</li><li>Thermoplastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass prepreg market is divided into two main types: Thermosetting and Thermoplastic. Thermosetting glass prepregs are commonly used due to their high heat resistance and ability to maintain structural integrity even at elevated temperatures. They are cured using heat and pressure, making them ideal for high-performance applications such as aerospace and automotive industries. On the other hand, thermoplastic glass prepregs can be reheated and reshaped multiple times, offering flexibility in manufacturing processes and reducing waste. They are often used in applications where recyclability and design flexibility are important.</p></p>
