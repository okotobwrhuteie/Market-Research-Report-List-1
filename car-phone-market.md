<p><p>The car phone market is divided into two main types based on network technology: CDMA and GSM. CDMA networks utilize a spread spectrum technology to transmit signal, offering secure and reliable communication. On the other hand, GSM networks use a time division technology for signal transmission, providing better voice quality and faster data speeds. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and geographical regions, with CDMA being more prevalent in North America and GSM dominating in Europe and Asia.</p></p>
