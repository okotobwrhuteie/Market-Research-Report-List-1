<p><p>Rainwater recycling systems can be categorized into three main types - direct-pumped type, gravity type, and others. Direct-pumped systems use a pump to distribute collected rainwater throughout the building for various uses. Gravity systems rely on the natural flow of water from higher to lower points, making them a cost-effective option. The "Others" category includes innovative solutions that may combine elements of both direct-pumped and gravity systems, offering unique features or functionalities to meet specific needs.</p></p>
