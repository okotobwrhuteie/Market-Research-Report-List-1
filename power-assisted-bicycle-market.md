<p><p>Power assisted bicycles, commonly known as e-bikes, come in two main types in the market: mid-drive and hub-drive. Mid-drive e-bikes have the motor situated in the middle of the bike frame, which provides a more balanced and efficient power transfer, making them suitable for hilly terrains and off-road riding. On the other hand, hub-drive e-bikes have the motor located in the wheel hub, which offers a smoother and quieter ride, making them popular for commuting and leisurely rides on flat terrain. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences in the e-bike market.</p></p>
