<p><ul><li>Ground-based Broadband</li><li>Satellite Broadband</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ground-based broadband for in-flight internet service uses ground stations to transmit signals up to the aircraft. This type of service is generally faster and more reliable but is limited by coverage areas. Satellite broadband for in-flight internet service uses satellite technology to provide connectivity regardless of location, making it ideal for long-haul flights. While satellite broadband may be slower and more expensive, it offers broader coverage compared to ground-based broadband. Both types cater to different needs within the in-flight broadband market.</p></p>
