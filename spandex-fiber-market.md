<p><ul><li>Solution Dry Spinning</li><li>Solution Wet Spinning</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spandex fiber market includes various types such as Solution Dry Spinning, Solution Wet Spinning, and Others. Solution Dry Spinning process involves dissolving spandex polymer in a solvent and then extruding it into fibers through air. Solution Wet Spinning, on the other hand, involves dissolving the polymer in a solvent and then extruding it into a coagulating bath. Other types of markets in the spandex fiber industry include melt spinning, reaction spinning, and gel spinning, which offer different benefits and applications for manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
