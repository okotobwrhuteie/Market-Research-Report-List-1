<p><p>Metal Cutting Machine (MCM) market is segmented into different types based on the technology used for cutting metal sheets. These types include Laser Cutting Machine, Waterjet Cutting Machine, Plasma Cutting Machine, and Flame Cutting Machine. Laser cutting machines use a laser beam to cut through materials, waterjet cutting machines use high-pressure water to cut through materials, plasma cutting machines use a high-velocity jet of ionized gas to cut materials, and flame cutting machines use a flame to heat and melt the metal for cutting. Other types of metal cutting machines include abrasive cutting machines and electrical discharge machining (EDM) machines.</p></p>
