<p><p>PVDF fluorocarbon coatings are commonly used in the market in three main types: powder coating, solvent coating, and waterborne coatings. Powder coating involves applying dry powder to a surface, then heat-curing it to form a hard finish. Solvent coating uses a solvent-based liquid to apply the coating, which evaporates as the coating dries. Waterborne coatings use water as a carrier for the coating, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
