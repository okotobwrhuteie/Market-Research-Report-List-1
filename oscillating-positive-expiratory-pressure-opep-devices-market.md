<p><p>Oscillating Positive Expiratory Pressure (OPEP) devices are handheld medical devices used to help clear mucus and improve lung function in patients with respiratory conditions such as Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD), asthma, cystic fibrosis, and bronchiectasis. These devices work by providing resistance during exhalation, which creates oscillations in the airways to help loosen and mobilize mucus, making it easier for patients to cough it up.</p><p>The OPEP devices market is experiencing steady growth, with a forecasted CAGR of 4.4% during the forecast period.
