<p><p>Biaxially Oriented Nylon Film Market consists of two main types - Sequential Stretching Type and Mechanical Simultaneous Stretching Type. In Sequential Stretching Type, the film is stretched first in one direction and then in another direction sequentially, resulting in improved mechanical properties. On the other hand, Mechanical Simultaneous Stretching Type involves stretching the film in both directions simultaneously, achieving higher productivity and consistency in properties. Both types offer unique benefits in various applications within the packaging and industrial sectors.</p></p>
