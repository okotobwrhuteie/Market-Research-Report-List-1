<p><ul><li>Mud Cap Drilling (MCD)</li><li>Dual Gradient Drilling (DGD)</li><li>Constant Bottom Hole Pressure (CBHP)</li><li>Return Flow Control Drilling (RFCD)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Managed Pressure Drilling (MPD) services market is segmented into different types based on technology and techniques used. Mud Cap Drilling (MCD) involves the use of heavier mud to control pressure. Dual Gradient Drilling (DGD) utilizes two fluids to manage pressure. Constant Bottom Hole Pressure (CBHP) maintains pressure throughout the wellbore. Return Flow Control Drilling (RFCD) regulates flow rate to control pressure. Other types include variations and combinations of these techniques to provide specialized solutions for challenging drilling conditions.</p></p>
