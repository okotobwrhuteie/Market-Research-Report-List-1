<p><p>Primary antioxidants are chemicals used to prevent the oxidation of materials such as plastics, rubbers, and fuels. Two main types are Amines and Hindered Phenols. Amines work by neutralizing free radicals, while Hindered Phenols inhibit the oxidation process by donating hydrogen atoms. Amines are commonly used in polyurethane foam and lubricants, while Hindered Phenols are used in plastics and rubber products. Both types are crucial in extending the lifespan and maintaining the quality of various materials by preventing degradation from oxidation.</p></p>
