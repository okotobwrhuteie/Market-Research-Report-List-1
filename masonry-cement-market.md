<p><p>Masonry cement is mainly available in three types: Type N, Type M, and Type S. Type N is commonly used for general construction purposes, Type M is known for its high compressive strength and is ideal for below-grade applications, and Type S is preferred for use in masonry structures subjected to high winds or seismic activity. Apart from these three main types, there are also other specialized types of masonry cement available based on specific project requirements or regional preferences. Each type offers unique properties and is suitable for different construction applications.</p></p>
