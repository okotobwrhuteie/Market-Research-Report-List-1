<p><p>Insulated Metal Substrate (IMS) PCB is a type of printed circuit board where a metallic substrate is used as an electrical isolation layer between the heat sink and the copper foil. This helps in dissipating heat efficiently, making it suitable for applications where thermal management is crucial, such as LED lighting, power converters, motor drives, and automotive electronics.</p><p>The Insulated Metal Substrate (IMS) PCB Market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for high-power density electronic devices with better thermal management capabilities. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.1% during the forecast period.
