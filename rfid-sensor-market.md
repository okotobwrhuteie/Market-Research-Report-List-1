<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The RFID Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency</li><li>High Frequency</li><li>Ultrahigh Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID sensor market is categorized into three types based on frequency range: Low Frequency (LF), High Frequency (HF), and Ultrahigh Frequency (UHF). LF RFID sensors operate in the range of 30 KHz to 300 KHz, HF operates at 3-30 MHz, and UHF operates at 300-3,000 MHz. LF sensors are known for short read ranges and lower data transfer rates, while UHF sensors offer longer read ranges and faster data transfer rates. HF sensors fall in between, offering a balance of read range and data transfer speed.</p></p>
