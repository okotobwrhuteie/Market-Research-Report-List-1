<p><p>Automated Lubrication Systems are mechanical devices that automatically deliver controlled amounts of lubricants to multiple lubrication points on a machine while it is in operation. There are two main types of lubrication systems: Grease Lubrication System and Oil Lubrication System. Grease Lubrication Systems use grease to lubricate machinery parts, while Oil Lubrication Systems use oil. Both systems are essential for maintaining machinery efficiency, reducing wear and tear, and preventing equipment breakdowns.</p></p>
