<p><strong>The Automotive Sunroof Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DC Brushed Motor</li><li>DC Brushless Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive sunroof motors can be categorized into two main types based on their technology: DC brushed motors and DC brushless motors. DC brushed motors use brushes to transfer electrical current to the rotor, causing it to turn. They are cost-effective and have high starting torque. On the other hand, DC brushless motors eliminate the need for brushes, resulting in improved efficiency, higher reliability, and longer lifespan. They are also quieter and require less maintenance compared to DC brushed motors.</p></p>
