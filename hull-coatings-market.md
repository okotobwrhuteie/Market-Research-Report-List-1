<p><strong>The Hull Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Polishing Coatings (SPCs)</li><li>Fouling Release Coatings (FRC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hull coatings market comprises two main types: Self-Polishing Coatings (SPCs) and Fouling Release Coatings (FRC). SPCs continuously leach biocides to prevent fouling, offering effective protection over time. FRCs create a slippery surface, making it difficult for marine organisms to attach and grow on the hull. While SPCs provide long-lasting protection, FRCs offer a more environmentally friendly option by reducing the need for biocides. Both types play a crucial role in preventing biofouling and enhancing vessel performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
