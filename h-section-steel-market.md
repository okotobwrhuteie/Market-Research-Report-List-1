<p><p>H-section steel is a type of structural steel commonly used in construction. There are two main types of H-section steel on the market: hot-rolled H-beams and welded H-beams. Hot-rolled H-beams are produced by heating a steel billet and passing it through rollers to create the desired shape. On the other hand, welded H-beams are made by welding together steel plates. Both types of H-section steel are widely used in construction due to their strength, durability, and versatility.</p></p>
