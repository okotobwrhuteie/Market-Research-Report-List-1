<p><p>Automotive Exhaust Heat Recovery Systems aim to increase fuel efficiency and reduce emissions by capturing waste heat from the exhaust system. Exhaust Gas Heat Recovery (EGHR) utilizes a heat exchanger to transfer heat from the exhaust gas to a working fluid. Rankine Cycle Systems use this fluid to generate power. Thermoelectric Generators convert temperature differentials into electricity. Electric Turbo Compounding (ETC) uses a generator connected to the turbocharger to recover energy from exhaust gases. These systems help vehicles operate more efficiently and sustainably.</p></p>
