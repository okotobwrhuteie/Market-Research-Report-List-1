<p><strong>The Polyolefin Pelletizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gear Pump Pelletizer</li><li>Extrusion Pelletizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyolefin pelletizers are categorized into two main types: Gear Pump Pelletizers and Extrusion Pelletizers. Gear Pump Pelletizer utilizes a gear pump to transport molten polymer through a die plate before cutting it into pellets. On the other hand, Extrusion Pelletizers involve the use of an extruder to force molten polymer through a die plate, followed by a cutting process to form pellets. Both types of pelletizers are widely used in the plastics industry for the efficient production of polyolefin pellets.</p></p>
