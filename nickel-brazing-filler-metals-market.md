<p><p>Nickel brazing filler metals are commonly available in various forms such as powder, brazing paste, tapes, foil, and preforms. Powder form is used for applications requiring high purity and fine particle size. Brazing paste offers easy application and uniform coating. Tapes are convenient for joining large surfaces. Each form caters to specific brazing requirements and applications.</p></p>
