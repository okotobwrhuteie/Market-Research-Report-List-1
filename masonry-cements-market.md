<p><p>Standard consistency masonry cements are designed for general use in masonry construction, providing a smooth, workable consistency for laying bricks and blocks. On the other hand, extra thick consistency masonry cements are specifically formulated for applications requiring a higher viscosity and bonding strength, such as structural constructions or where high durability is needed. Both types of masonry cements play a crucial role in providing different levels of consistency and strength to meet the specific requirements of various construction projects.</p></p>
