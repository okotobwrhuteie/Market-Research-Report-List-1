<p><ul><li>Dehydrated Food</li><li>Freeze-dried Food</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Long Term Food Storage market types include dehydrated food and freeze-dried food. Dehydrated food involves removing water from the food item through a drying process, which extends its shelf life. Freeze-dried food is preserved through a process of freezing the food and then removing the ice crystals under vacuum pressure, retaining the food's original texture, flavor, and nutrients. Both types of food storage are popular choices for long-term emergency preparedness, backpacking, and camping due to their lightweight, compact nature and extended shelf life.</p></p>
