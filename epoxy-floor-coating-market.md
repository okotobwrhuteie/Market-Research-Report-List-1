<p><ul><li>Solvent Epoxy Floor Coating</li><li>Non-Solvent Epoxy Floor Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Epoxy floor coatings are available in two main types: solvent-based and non-solvent-based. Solvent epoxy floor coatings contain chemicals that evaporate during the drying process, while non-solvent epoxy floor coatings are environmentally friendly and do not require harmful chemicals. Solvent-based coatings are known for their faster drying times and stronger adhesion, while non-solvent coatings are preferred for their low VOC emissions and safer application. Both types offer durable protection and a glossy finish for concrete surfaces.</p></p>
