<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Mechanical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Above-ground pool timers can be classified into two main types: electric and mechanical. Electric timers are controlled by an electrical circuit and can be programmed to turn the pool equipment on and off at specific times. They offer more precision and flexibility in scheduling. On the other hand, mechanical timers use gears and pins to control the on/off schedule and are simpler to operate. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with electric timers being more popular for their advanced features and convenience, while mechanical timers are favored for their reliability and durability.</p></p>
