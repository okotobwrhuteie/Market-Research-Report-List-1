<p><p>The electric two-wheeler charging station market consists of two main types: AC and DC. AC charging stations deliver power to the vehicle using alternating current, while DC charging stations provide power through direct current. AC charging stations are more common and can be found in homes, workplaces, and public areas, offering slower charging speeds. DC charging stations, on the other hand, are typically faster and are commonly installed along highways or in high-traffic areas. Both types play a crucial role in supporting the growth of electric two-wheeler usage.</p></p>
