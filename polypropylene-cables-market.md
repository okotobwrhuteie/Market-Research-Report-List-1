<p><p>Polypropylene cables are commonly used in the telecommunications industry for their high tensile strength and resistance to moisture. There are two main types of polypropylene cables in the market: multi-mode and single-mode. Multi-mode cables are designed for shorter distances and have a larger core size, allowing multiple light rays to travel through at the same time. Single-mode cables, on the other hand, are designed for longer distances and have a smaller core size, allowing only a single light ray to travel through, resulting in higher transmission speeds.</p></p>
