<p><p>Bio-based thermoplastic elastomers are divided into two main types: synthesized bio-based polyester elastomer and natural bio-based polyester elastomer. The synthesized bio-based polyester elastomer is derived from bio-based materials through a chemical synthesis process, offering enhanced properties and customization options. On the other hand, natural bio-based polyester elastomers are directly extracted from natural sources such as plants, making them more environmentally friendly and sustainable. Both types are gaining popularity in the market due to their biodegradability and reduced carbon footprint compared to traditional petroleum-based elastomers.</p></p>
