<p><p>Wafer-level packaging equipment market consists of various types including Fan In, Fan Out, and Others. Fan In packaging involves mounting the die face-down on the substrate, while Fan Out packaging involves repackaging multiple die into a larger package. Other types of packaging techniques include Through Silicon Via (TSV), molded wafer level packages (WLP), and chip-on-wafer (CoW). Each type offers unique benefits and functionalities, catering to different industry requirements and applications.</p></p>
