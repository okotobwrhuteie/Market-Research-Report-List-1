<p><p>Vanilla comes in various market types such as bean, extract, powder, and paste. Vanilla beans are the whole, unprocessed pods containing the flavorful black seeds. Extract is a concentrated liquid form made by soaking vanilla beans in alcohol. Vanilla powder is a dry form made by grinding dried vanilla beans. Vanilla paste is a combination of vanilla extract and vanilla bean seeds. Each market type offers a different intensity of flavor and is used for various culinary purposes such as baking, cooking, and flavoring beverages.</p></p>
