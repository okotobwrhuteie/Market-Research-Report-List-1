<p><ul><li>AFS</li><li>ABD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart Adaptive Headlights in the automotive industry encompass various technologies such as Adaptive Front-lighting System (AFS) and Adaptive Beam Control (ABD). AFS adjusts the direction and range of the headlights based on the vehicle's speed, steering angle, and driving conditions, increasing visibility and safety. ABD further enhances this by selectively dimming certain portions of the light beam to prevent blinding other drivers while still providing optimal illumination. Both technologies aim to improve driver visibility and overall road safety, making driving at night or in adverse conditions safer and more comfortable.</p></p>
