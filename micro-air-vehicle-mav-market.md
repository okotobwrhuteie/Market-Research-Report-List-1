<p><p>The Micro Air Vehicle (MAV) market consists of two main types: Fixed Wing and Rotary Wing. Fixed Wing MAVs are designed with a stable and efficient aerodynamic configuration for longer flight endurance and higher speeds. They are generally used for surveillance, mapping, and monitoring applications. On the other hand, Rotary Wing MAVs have the advantage of vertical takeoff and landing capabilities, making them ideal for applications that require maneuverability and hovering. They are commonly used for tasks like search and rescue, inspection, and photography.</p></p>
