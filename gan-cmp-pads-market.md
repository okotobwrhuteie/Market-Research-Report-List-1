<p><strong>The GaN CMP Pads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hard Pads</li><li>Soft Pads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GaN CMP pads can be categorized into two main types: hard pads and soft pads. Hard pads are designed for applications requiring more aggressive material removal, providing higher material removal rates. Soft pads, on the other hand, are suitable for applications that require finer finishing and higher planarity. Hard pads are typically used for rough polishing stages, while soft pads are used for final polishing stages. Both types of pads play a crucial role in the CMP process for GaN materials.</p></p>
