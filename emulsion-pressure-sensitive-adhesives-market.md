<p><p>Emulsion Pressure Sensitive Adhesives are a type of adhesive that forms a bond when pressure is applied. The three main types of emulsion pressure sensitive adhesives on the market are Acrylic, PVA (polyvinyl acetate), and EVA (ethylene vinyl acetate). Acrylic adhesives are known for their high performance and durability, making them ideal for a wide range of applications. PVA adhesives are commonly used in woodworking and paper crafts due to their ease of use and affordability. EVA adhesives are flexible and resistant to aging, making them suitable for applications requiring elasticity.</p></p>
