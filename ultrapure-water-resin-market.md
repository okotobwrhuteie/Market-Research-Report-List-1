<p><p>Ultrapure water resin market consists of three main types: mixed resins, anion resins, and cationic resins. Mixed resins are a combination of both anion and cation exchange resins, allowing for efficient removal of impurities in water. Anion resins specifically target negatively charged ions, while cationic resins target positively charged ions. Each type of resin plays a critical role in the purification process, ensuring the production of high-quality ultrapure water for various industrial applications.</p></p>
