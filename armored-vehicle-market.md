<p><p>Armored vehicles are divided into two main types, wheeled and tracked. Wheeled armored vehicles, as the name suggests, have wheels for mobility and are suitable for use in a variety of terrains. They offer speed and versatility, making them ideal for reconnaissance and support roles. Tracked armored vehicles, on the other hand, use tracks for propulsion, providing superior off-road performance and maneuverability. They are commonly used for heavier combat roles due to their increased stability and ability to navigate rough terrain.</p></p>
