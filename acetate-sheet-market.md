<p><ul><li>Platen</li><li>Squeeze Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acetate sheet market consists of two main types: Platen and Squeeze Board. Platen acetate sheets are flat sheets designed for use in various applications such as packaging, medical devices, and graphic arts. They are known for their durability and flexibility. On the other hand, Squeeze Board acetate sheets are specialized sheets used for embossing and shaping materials such as leather, paper, and fabric. They are typically thicker than platen sheets and are specifically designed for squeezing and pressing operations.</p></p>
