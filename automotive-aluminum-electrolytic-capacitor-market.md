<p><p>Automotive aluminum electrolytic capacitors come in various types to suit different applications. Axial capacitors have leads coming from opposite ends, while radial crown capacitors have leads on one side. Snap-in capacitors are mounted onto a PCB with metal tabs. V-chip capacitors are compact and rectangular in shape. Other types may include SMD capacitors and screw terminal capacitors. Each type is designed for specific mounting and space requirements in automotive electronic systems.</p></p>
