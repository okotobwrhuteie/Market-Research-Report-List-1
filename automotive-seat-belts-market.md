Inflatable Seat Belts feature airbags within the seat belt to provide added protection in the event of a crash. Common Belts are traditional seat belts without any additional features. Single-stage Belt Force Limiters are equipped with technology that reduces the force exerted on the occupant during a collision, helping to minimize injuries. These types cater to different safety needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
