<p><p>Counter-flow heat exchangers come in various types. Plate heat exchangers use metal plates to transfer heat between two fluids. Shell and tube heat exchangers have tubes inside a shell to facilitate heat exchange. Double-pipe heat exchangers consist of two pipes, one inside the other, for efficient heat transfer. Other types include spiral heat exchangers, finned tube heat exchangers, and air-cooled heat exchangers, each designed for specific applications. These market types cater to diverse industrial needs for efficient heat transfer processes.</p></p>
