<p><p>Power transmission and motion control market includes various types such as motors, gearings, clutches & brakes, belts & chain drivers, hydraulics/pneumatics, and others. Motors are devices that convert electrical energy into mechanical energy. Gearings are used to transmit motion between the shafts of different components. Clutches & brakes control the transmission of power between moving parts. Belts & chain drivers are used to transfer power between rotating shafts. Hydraulics/Pneumatics use fluids and gases to transmit power. Other types include couplings, bearings, and actuators.</p></p>
