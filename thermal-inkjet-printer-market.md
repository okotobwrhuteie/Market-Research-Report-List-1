<p><p>Thermal inkjet printers are classified into two types based on the shooting direction of ink droplets - Roof Shooter Thermal Inkjet Printer and Side Shooter Thermal Inkjet Printer. Roof shooter printers shoot ink droplets from the top of the print head, while side shooter printers shoot ink droplets from the side of the print head. Both types of printers have their own advantages and are suitable for different applications based on factors such as print speed, print quality, and precision.</p></p>
