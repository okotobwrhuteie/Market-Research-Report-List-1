<p><p>Veterinary amino acids feed additives market can be categorized into two types: Essential Amino Acids and Non-Essential Amino Acids. Essential amino acids are those that cannot be synthesized by animals and must be obtained through their diet. Non-essential amino acids, on the other hand, can be synthesized by animals in adequate amounts. Both types of amino acids play a crucial role in animal nutrition and health, promoting growth, muscle development, and overall well-being in veterinary species.</p></p>
