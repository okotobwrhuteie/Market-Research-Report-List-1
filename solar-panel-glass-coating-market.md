<p><p>Solar panel glass coatings can be classified into two main types - hydrophilic and hydrophobic. Hydrophilic coatings are designed to attract water molecules, spreading them evenly across the surface to promote better water flow and self-cleaning properties. On the other hand, hydrophobic coatings repel water, causing droplets to bead up and roll off the surface, preventing water spots and debris buildup. Both types of coatings serve to improve the efficiency and longevity of solar panels by keeping them clean and free from environmental contaminants.</p></p>
