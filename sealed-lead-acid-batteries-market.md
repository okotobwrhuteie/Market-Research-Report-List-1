<p><p>Sealed Lead-Acid Batteries are classified into different types based on their construction and electrolyte materials. Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) Batteries use a fiberglass mat to absorb the electrolyte, allowing for maintenance-free operation. Gel Batteries use silica to immobilize the electrolyte, providing a longer lifespan and enhanced performance. Other types of Sealed Lead-Acid Batteries include flooded lead-acid batteries and valve-regulated lead-acid batteries. Each type offers specific benefits and is used in various applications depending on the requirements of the system.</p></p>
