<p><p>Solar PERC PV modules are advanced solar panels that use Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell (PERC) technology. There are two main types of PERC cells in the market - Mono PERC Cells and Poly PERC Cells. Mono PERC cells are made from monocrystalline silicon, offering higher efficiency and better performance in low light conditions. Poly PERC cells, on the other hand, are made from polycrystalline silicon, providing a more cost-effective option while still offering decent efficiency levels. Both types are popular choices for residential and commercial solar installations.</p></p>
