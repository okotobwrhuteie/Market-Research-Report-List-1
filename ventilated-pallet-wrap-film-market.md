<p><p>Ventilated Pallet Wrap Film is available in two main types: Manual Stretch Film and Machine Stretch Film. Manual Stretch Film is applied by hand and is ideal for smaller quantities or irregularly shaped pallets. Machine Stretch Film is designed to be used with a pallet wrapping machine, making it more efficient for larger scale operations. Both types of film offer ventilation to allow air circulation and prevent condensation, protecting goods from moisture damage during transportation and storage.</p></p>
