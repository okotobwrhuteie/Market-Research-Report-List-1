<p><p>The GaAs polished wafer market consists of three main types: LEC Grown GaAs, VGF Grown GaAs, and Others. LEC Grown GaAs wafers are produced using the Liquid Encapsulated Czochralski method, resulting in high purity and low defect levels. VGF Grown GaAs wafers are grown using the Vertical Gradient Freeze technique, known for its high crystal quality and uniformity. The "Others" category includes GaAs wafers grown using alternative methods, such as MOCVD or MBE, offering different properties and applications than the traditional LEC and VGF methods.</p></p>
