<p><p>Honeycomb paperboard market is divided into three main types: Continuous Paper Honeycomb, Blocks Paper Honeycomb, and Expanded Paper Honeycomb. Continuous Paper Honeycomb is manufactured in a continuous process, allowing for long lengths of honeycomb structure. Blocks Paper Honeycomb consists of individual blocks or cells of paperboard arranged in a honeycomb pattern. Expanded Paper Honeycomb is created by expanding the paperboard to form a lightweight and strong material ideal for packaging and construction applications. Each type offers unique properties and is utilized in various industries.</p></p>
