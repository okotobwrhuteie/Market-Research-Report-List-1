<p><p>Insulation sealant market types can be classified into organic gum and inorganic glue. Organic gum sealants are made from natural materials such as rubber or resin. They offer great flexibility, adhesion, and durability. Inorganic glue sealants are typically made from synthetic materials like silicone or acrylic. They are known for their water resistance, heat resistance, and chemical stability. Both types of sealants play a crucial role in providing insulation and protecting surfaces from moisture, heat, and other environmental factors.</p></p>
