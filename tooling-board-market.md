<p><p>Tooling boards are commonly classified into two main types based on their composition: polyurethane and epoxy boards. Polyurethane tooling boards are known for their lightweight and easy machinability, making them ideal for short-run production and prototyping applications. On the other hand, epoxy tooling boards are preferred for their high stability and heat resistance, making them suitable for long-term tooling and production processes. Both types of tooling boards offer high dimensional accuracy and surface finish, making them popular choices in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and marine.</p></p>
