<p><p>Low-flush toilets are designed to conserve water by using less water per flush compared to traditional toilets. There are two main types in the market: single-flush and dual-flush. Single-flush toilets have one flushing option which releases a set amount of water with each flush. On the other hand, dual-flush toilets offer two flushing options - a lower flush for liquid waste and a higher flush for solid waste. This allows users to choose the appropriate amount of water needed for each flush, further increasing water conservation efforts.</p></p>
