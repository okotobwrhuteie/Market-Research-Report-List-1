<p><ul><li>Colloidal Silica Polishing Slurry</li><li>Alumina Polishing Slurry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GaN CMP slurry market offers two main types of CMP slurries: Colloidal silica polishing slurry and alumina polishing slurry. Colloidal silica polishing slurry is widely used for its high removal rate and selectivity, making it suitable for planarization of various substrates. On the other hand, alumina polishing slurry is known for its excellent oxide layer removal performance and high chemical stability. Both types of slurries play crucial roles in the semiconductor industry for polishing and smoothing GaN surfaces to achieve optimal device performance.</p></p>
